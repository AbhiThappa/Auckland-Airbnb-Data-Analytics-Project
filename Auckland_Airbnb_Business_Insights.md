# Auckland Airbnb Analytics - Business Insights Report

## Executive Summary

This report summarises key findings from an end-to-end Airbnb data
analytics project developed using Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI. The
dashboard analyses listing performance, pricing, occupancy, host
performance, customer ratings, and neighbourhood-level revenue across
Auckland.

## Key Insights

1.  **Townhouses are the top revenue-generating property type**,
    producing approximately **\$1.0M** in revenue, followed by Houses
    (\~\$0.89M) and Apartments (\~\$0.56M).

2.  **Mission Bay is the highest revenue-generating neighbourhood**
    (\~\$0.67M), followed by Ponsonby (\~\$0.55M), indicating these
    locations have the strongest earning potential.

3.  **Average occupancy is 65.42%**, suggesting healthy demand while
    leaving opportunities to increase booking utilisation.

4.  **Average nightly price is approximately \$360**, positioning many
    Auckland listings within the premium accommodation market.

5.  **Average guest rating is 4.18/5**, indicating positive guest
    experiences overall.

6.  **The dataset contains 50 listings and more than 8,200 reviews**,
    providing a reliable sample for performance analysis.

7.  **Sophia is the highest revenue-generating host**, with Ava and
    Mason following closely behind.

8.  **Sophia also records the highest average guest rating**,
    demonstrating that excellent guest satisfaction can align with
    strong financial performance.

9.  **Average host response rate is approximately 86%**, indicating good
    responsiveness across hosts.

10. **Townhouses achieve the highest occupancy rate (78.7%)**, while
    Apartments show significantly lower occupancy (42.4%).

11. **Herne Bay has the highest average nightly price**, reflecting its
    premium market positioning.

12. **St Heliers records the highest occupancy (95.8%)**, highlighting
    exceptionally strong booking demand.

13. **Ponsonby combines strong occupancy with high revenue**, making it
    one of the most balanced and profitable neighbourhoods.

14. **Neighbourhood ratings remain above 4.0**, indicating consistently
    positive guest satisfaction.

15. **Parnell records the lowest average rating among the displayed
    neighbourhoods**, presenting an opportunity to improve guest
    experience.

## Business Recommendations

-   Increase marketing investment in Mission Bay and Ponsonby.
-   Expand townhouse listings where possible due to their strong revenue
    performance.
-   Benchmark top-performing hosts and share best practices.
-   Review pricing strategies alongside occupancy trends rather than
    relying on price alone.
-   Improve lower-rated listings through enhanced amenities,
    cleanliness, and guest communication.
-   Monitor occupancy and revenue together to optimise pricing
    decisions.
